What We Do

Everything We Do Is
Built For Young Builders.

Youngpreneurs Club is a community-driven podcast built entirely for young people who want to start something real. We bring startup CEOs to the table and ask them what no one else dares to ask — so you get the real roadmap, not the highlight reel.

🎙️
Podcast With Startup CEOs

Every episode we sit down with a real startup founder and go deep — their journey from zero, how they built their company, the investors, the team, and the product. All of it, unfiltered.

🕶️
Revealing the Dark Reality

We don't just cover the wins. We dig into the failures, the burnout, the co-founder conflicts, the moments they almost quit — the truth behind the startup world nobody talks about publicly.

🗺️
Understanding the Journey

We map out the full startup journey — from the idea stage to growth, funding, and scale. So you understand every phase before you step into it, not after it's already too late.

🔑
Unlocking the Real Answers

We ask the questions you actually have. How did you get your first customer? What made you survive when others didn't? Direct, honest answers from people who've lived it.

🏆
How to Become Successful

Every episode ends with the founder's single most important lesson. Not theory, not motivational fluff — the actual thing that moved the needle for them, distilled for you.

🚀
Helping You Build a Startup

Youngpreneurs Club exists to give young people the knowledge, courage, and community to build. No school required. No degree required. Just hunger, and the willingness to start.

Why We Exist

The World Teaches You
What to Think.
We Show You
How to Build.

Every year, thousands of young people have ideas — real, powerful, world-changing ideas. But nobody around them has ever built a startup. No mentor. No guide. No one to ask "is this normal?" or "what do I do next?"

So they scroll through highlight reels on social media. They see the funding announcements, the Forbes covers, the TED Talk moments. And they think — "maybe I'm not cut out for this."

That's exactly why Youngpreneurs Club was built. To pull back the curtain. To sit across from the people who've actually done it and ask them the real questions — so you know the truth before you start, not after you've already given up.

Not a School

No lectures, no syllabus, no degrees. Just real conversations with people who built something from nothing — and the lessons they paid for with years of their life.

Not a Highlight Reel

We don't do PR interviews. Every founder we bring on talks about the failures, the dark moments, the things that almost ended everything — because that's where the real lessons live.

Built For Young People

Youngpreneurs Club exists specifically for young builders who are hungry, have no roadmap, and refuse to wait for permission. You don't need experience. You need the truth.

A Community, Not a Course

We're building a club — a place where young founders find each other, share ideas, get honest feedback, and grow together. No fee. No gatekeeping. Just builders helping builders.
